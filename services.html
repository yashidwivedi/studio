Services

We believe in Quintessential Planning.

Every event defines the bond between the client and the event planner. We like to nurture that bond and make it stronger with our creativity and direction. The most memorable events come together when talented planners & suppliers work seamlessly together.

We thrive to find the perfect venue to celebrate your day, frame menu that your guest will love and fashion an atmosphere that represents your personality through our planning.

Locus Quest Every event has the need for the right venue. Every venue tells the story of the choice we make. Our team curates the most luxurious and bespoke venues that fit the vision and serve as the perfect backdrop for your celebrations.
Budget Intendance Calculations become daunting at times, especially when it’s as important as your celebration. These figures play a significant role in decisions like venue, guest lists, meal options and of course the attempt to spring that pricey DJ. It’s essential to account for the right numbers for which our team is at your service to serve you best.
Hospitality & RSVP We cast and design personalised services for each attending guest making them feel the #DreamKnotsTouch. Beginning from RSVP confirmations, guest arrivals, their stay and their departure plus everything in between. Each element is taken into consideration while planning the best experience for your guests along with all logistical requirements.
The Invite The first impression of your wedding is created with a stunning invite. We help you design, personalise and print ravishing invitations and stationery that will enlighten your wedding. We want to carve the path on this journey to serve as your memory lane.
Logistic Stewardship Our team understands each concern carefully and gives the optimum results for you. From reckoning the cab services for guests arrival and departure to keeping the compact coordination with drivers. Car allocation for guests, paging for pickups & escorting guests, everything is managed under one roof.
Artist & Merchant Management Dream Knots is here to ensure that your wedding day is memorable. From giving artist options to getting them onboard, we provide luxury planning services to discerning couples. We will patch you with the right vendors and chaperon their services working tirelessly to make your event a picturesque reality.
The Set Like in a movie, the right set is key. Every celebration needs an apt design for decor. Our team of experts indulge in conceptualising an aesthetically appropriate decor for your vision with the perfect lights, florals and themes.
Crafting The Menu Tingling the taste buds and creating a memory with the right menu is a vital part of a wedding. We help you design, style and create the perfect food & bar menu for your celebrations.
