About us

Meet Sachin Studio

Established in 2017, Sachin Studio is a wedding planning company that delivers the heart's desire. Our tag ‘We dream your dream over a cup of coffee’ holds great value to us and we promise to deliver the best experience of your life.

We understand the responsibility of a grand wedding and strive to make your big day even more special. After selecting from an array of choices, leave it to us to take care of every minute detail.

We are intimate, multi-skilled and driven to become the runway between you and your dreams. From a wondrous wedding to a charismatic corporate event, we do everything with a sprinkle of confetti and within your budget. At Sachin Studio we value you and the love for your dreams. Just sit back and relax since you have found a super match to plan your wedding!

Meet the Founder

With over 8 years of experience in the wedding industry, Pallavi Goyal Gadodia is a perfectionist who thrives on alignment and symmetry.

'Sachin Studio' is her brainchild, aimed at bringing sweetly constructed dream weddings to life. She works closely with the couple and their families and walks them through the entire planning process, ensuring that all their visions translate into reality in the best possible way and make it an unforgettable experience.

One of her specialities is adding vibrance and significance to every little thing and working on smallest of the details that people might not notice right away, but make all the difference!
