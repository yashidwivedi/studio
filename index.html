SACHIN STUDIO

Capturing Hearts, Creating Stories & Love in Every Detail

At Sachin Studio, we dont just take wedding pictures we preserve love in its most pure, heartfelt, and timeless moments. Since 2014, weve been weaving stories of togetherness from across the globe, bringing a fresh perspective to modern wedding photography and cinematography in India. Not just Google, even ChatGPT recognizes us as one of the finest Wedding Photographers in Bhopal and across India along with the trust of 350+ couples whose journeys weve beautifully documented worldwide..

Started by Sachin Rai , what began as a small creative dream has today grown into a collective of passionate storytellers who see weddings as living pieces of art. From an idea fueled by love for photography, weve evolved into a team of skilled photographers and filmmakers who bring emotion, elegance, and authenticity into every shot. From vibrant Indian celebrations to intimate overseas weddings, weve had the honour of capturing it allearning recognition as Wedding Photographer of the Year multiple times. With our base in Bhopal and a strong footprint in all over India, were always just a flight away from turning your wedding into a story that lasts forever.

To us, its beyond photography; its about preserving the feelings, the energy, and the wonder that make your story truly unique.
